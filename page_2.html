Food Pantry at Umich

College students and staff are experiencing food insecurity at alarming rates. The Maize and Blue Cupboard is here to provide an immediate and comprehensive response for the U-M community. By offering resources, educational opportunities, compassionate support and more, they help students develop the skills to make informed decisions.

What they provide

  1. FOOD: Produce, dairy, meat, bread, frozen and shelf-stable foods (i.e. rice, cans, tuna, etc.)
  2. KITCHEN & COOKING: Dishes, silverware, pots & pans, Tupperware, cutting boards, knives, and other kitchenware
  3. PERSONAL & HOUSEHOLD: Trash bags, toilet paper, cleaning supplies, hygiene products, school supplies, baby items and more
  4. SUPPORT: Get connected to campus experts like CAPS, Dean of Students and Financial Aid, as well as SNAP (Michiganâ€™s food program)
